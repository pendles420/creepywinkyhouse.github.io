It's really me

A hug makes me feel connected to the earth.

I hope one day, I don’t feel like I ruin everything I touch.

I will keep my mask off even if that means living in peace by myself. Even if that means dying alone.

I have good memories I can hold onto.

I have things around me that remind me of my loved ones even if they passed away or I don’t see them or talk to them.

The horse on canvas is my trophy of peace. It served its purpose, it can go back to Goodwill when it’s ready.

4-H gives my family stability & a community.

Sunny Carroll is a mother. She loves nature. She spends her free time with clouds and trees. Her newest found friend is an owl in her canyon. Every night that owl is there, every night.
